about us

Kayee was founded by Mr. Sam Tsai in September 1992 with a compliment of 3 people based in Taipei, under his impetus, vision and direction Kayee has continued to grow and gain recognition and trust from its customers and suppliers. Strengthened by its unique experience, worldwide net working and overall good commercial marketing sense, Kayee has continued successfully to increase its turnover in a structured manner each year. Kayee roots in mail order still remain a solid part of the companies core activities alongside other fast growing global markets including "as seen on TV", retail and internet sales. With over 15 years experience and co-operation with many reputable manufacturers Kayee continues to develop or co-develop new exciting and innovative products to match customer expectations and meet rapid changing market demands and high global competition. Kayee U.S.P.(unique selling proposition) to its customers strives to deliver a 360 service including product, price and presentation across its wide spectrum of product categories and marketing activities, including D.R.T.V., mail order, retail, internet and promotional sales, and our close working relationships with our customers enables us to deliver this.

With offices in Taipei, Shenzhen and Ningbo with over 200 staff including dedicated R&D, QC, engineering, tooling, merchandise, sales and operational departments, Kayee is well placed and fully equipped to meet the high demands of our customers in the fast moving, ever changing market dynamics of today.
