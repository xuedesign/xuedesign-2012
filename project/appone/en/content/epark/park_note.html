Must knowns before applying to reside in the Innoative incubation lab

Online registration Must knowns Orgin of project

1、The object and purpose of the Innovative Incubation Lab

Due to community sharing, cloud applications and business operations rising from  the rapid changes of consumer behavior, The trend of "sharing economy" (Shareconomy) is forming worldwide.  In order to assist the rapid industry development and the promotion of mutual aid,  individual teams are here to share economy-type field to promote domestic entrepreneurship  in  the App industry ,especially with the establishment of the Innovative Incubation Lab, generating ideas, and create App to benefit mutually, and create inter-industry atmosphere, internal facilitate exchanges between the teams, sharing foreign assistance, App development support services towards the various industries, thus promoting industrial restructuring and upgrading service.

The object and purpose of the Innovative Incubation Lab include:

  •  APP development team to promote domestic resources and mutual assistance between each other, collaboration, sharing of successful models and conduct exchanges diffusion across counties of Taiwan.
  • Introducing and open government and public sector information and developed App, provide product information to allow team interface and innovative development for reuse of resources.
  • To publicly share cooperative business model, allowing innovation and fresh resources to help young entrepreneurs.
  • Training team used to create knowledge exchange, develop innovative services for scientific and technological exchanges to promote mutual cooperation mechanism, resources to perform the most efficient industrial applications.

2、Qualification of the team we recruited

  • For those who are eligibility: App development expert and those who already have a product, willing to devote their resources within the Innovation Incubation Lab (you can choose from one of the following):
    • Self-developed technology that will be at least be an API or SDK available for use to share and exchange Unit-related assistance, technical categories such as 3D, NFC, mobile payments, etc., but not be limited to, can be adjusted with the current status. (Note 1)
    • Other, teams, need to propose what specific items or technology, that the team can assist, in other related measures, such as assisting in product integrity testing products and services, give suggestions to help other team marketing products and services, to help develop courses related in App courses, The stationed review board will review the contribution and make the decision.
  • Stationed topics: A digital learning content, various kinds of digital content, digital media and aiming towards the three industries Four Modernizations policy (Please look into Taiwan's industrial structure optimization - three four modernizations industry specific action plan) of each field, type, and development team.

3、Eligibility Conditions

  • 各All kinds Apps engaged in product development, and those who has been added to our products or services, individuals, teams (individual or team leader must R.O.C. citizen or resident) or company, studios established or registered in the Republic of China, for those companies that has App development performance.
  • We expected to open up to 15 group to be stationed within this space, after review by the park, resource allocation towards individual open space, current situations, each team has a maximum number of five personnel.

4、The Innovation Incubation Lab offers the following services

  • Professional online portal services, and a platform for teams to release their product information, technical resources, forums, and other related services.
  • Providing local government Open data, the team sharing an open API and other resources to assist the team to quickly adopt towards commercial usage. （Note 2）
  • To provide distance learning & teaching, course in teaching all kinds of technology, video conferencing and other sharing mechanism to help promote the spread of cross-regional team, to facilitate the successful business model replication.
  • Provide toward those who are stationed within this space and related facilities (provided by the status of the park).
  • On occasion, interdisciplinary exchanges in various fields, such as providing industry knowledge and project requirements.
  • The Innovative Incubation Lab allows for visits, shows, exhibitions, conferences and other marketing communication competition or promotion to promote awareness.
  • Government subsidy program in counseling and support.
  • Technology development, market information, patents and legal counseling and other related fields.

5、Individual / Team stationed within the period and the rights and obligations

  • Should comply with the relevant requirements of the park, and towards the park aforementioned "sharing economy" object and purpose.
  • A period of three months is consider one term, after completion of the term, careful examination by the review board may be extended to up to two extensions.
  • Shall comply with the relevant requirements of the Innovation Incubation Lab.
  • The rights and obligations while be regulated by the contract.
  • Teams are to participate in each other's products testing process within the park, and provide feedback to the other teams.
  • Co work with other cities and the county park in remote locations, to allow for teaching of other team, suggestions and guidances, etc. External video sharing service.
  • The park will measure team teaching results, sharing of API, cumulative frequency of the contribution, as the next phase criteria of evaluation of their terms.
