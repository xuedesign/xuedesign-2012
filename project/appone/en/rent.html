Leasing & rental of testing equipments


  • Terms of Use
  • Tablet PC
  • Notebook
  • Mobile phone
  • EEG equipment
  1. The following usage rules & regulations applies towards the rental & leasing of the equipment belonging to the Institute for Information Industry (III) (hereinafter referred to as III) Digital Education Institute (hereinafter referred to as teaching and research institution) on its ownership and management of its public facilities.
  2. For those residing teams, who wish to apply for the usage of the devices, one must make a reservation, towards one’s team counselor, and fill out the application form to borrow, rent or lease these equipments.For EEG equipment please makes a reservation with Miss Xiang Chen: stephchen@iii.org.tw)
  3. The period borrowing period is for one week only (including weekends, holidays), if there are no other reservation for these equipments, one can renews the borrowing of the equipment, for one time only.
  4. Prior equipment hand over, equipment managers and borrower must count & re-check the quantity & conditions of all equipment & its accessories. To be sure equipment status, quality & quantity are well noted on the borrowing application form, signature signed by the borrower. Upon returning the equipments, the device manager will personally inspect all equipment condition & quantity, with a written signature for confirmation.
  5. The borrower of the equipment is responsible for the custody of the equipment, if there are any lost, destroyed or damaged conditions towards the equipment, the borrower shall be liable for all costs related in repairing expenses and restoring of the equipments.
