How to Use NFC Adapter in PassPac App

The Features of NFC Adapter

  • Dimension: 60X40X13 mm
  • Battery: 3.7V 95MAH
  • Charging: Micro USB 5 pin
  • Interface: Audio jack 3.5mm 4 pole connector/Manchester encoded UART data stream
  • Operating Frequency:@13.56MHz
  • OS: Support iOS & Android
  • Read Distance: < 3 cm
  • Air Protocol: ISO14443A/ISO 15693
  • Function: Tag Mode(64Sector&KeyA/B, without SE) Reader Mode
  • Color: Colorful Mobile phone strap Design
